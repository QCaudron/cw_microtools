CW Microtools

This is a small collection of tools for those learning CW.
Word Generator Generate random words from a subset of letters.
Sentence Generator Generate sentences from a subset of letters.
LICW QSO Protocol Generator Work through the LICW QSO Protocol.
Contest Protocol Generator Run the LICW Challenge, the SKCC WES, the K1USN SST, SOTA, or Field Day.
WPM and Dit Speed Calculator Calculate your speed from how long your dits are, or vice versa.
QRQ Visual Copy Exercise your QRQ word-building, visually.
